Creating a comprehensive EMS field guide and educational app for EMTs, AEMTs, and Paramedics requires a well-organized, feature-rich platform. Below is a detailed breakdown of everything the app should include:

---

### **1. Drug Reference Guide**
- **Drug List**: Comprehensive list of all medications used in pre-hospital care (e.g., epinephrine, nitroglycerin, fentanyl, amiodarone).
- **Indications**: When to administer each drug.
- **Contraindications**: When NOT to administer.
- **Dosages**: Adult/Pediatric dosages (mg/kg where applicable).
- **Routes of Administration**: IV/IO/IM/IN/PO/SL.
- **Side Effects/Adverse Reactions**.
- **Mechanism of Action** (for Paramedic-level understanding).
- **Compatibility/Incompatibility** (e.g., can it be mixed with other drugs?).
- **Onset/Duration** of action.
- **Special Considerations** (e.g., pregnancy, geriatrics, pediatrics).

---

### **2. Medical Conditions & Protocols**
- **Signs/Symptoms**: Organized by system (cardiac, respiratory, neuro, etc.).
- **Differential Diagnoses**: Help narrow down possible conditions.
- **Step-by-Step Interventions**: Based on local/National EMS Guidelines (e.g., NREMT, AHA).
- **Protocols for**:
  - Cardiac Arrest (ACLS/PALS)
  - Stroke (FAST/BEFAST, LVO stroke)
  - Trauma (TCCC, hemorrhage control)
  - OB/GYN (delivery, eclampsia)
  - Pediatric Emergencies
  - Behavioral Emergencies
  - Toxicology/Overdose
  - Infectious Diseases (Sepsis, COVID-19)

---

### **3. Assessment Tools**
- **Primary & Secondary Survey** (ABCDE approach).
- **OPQRST/SAMPLE** history-taking.
- **Vital Signs Ranges** (Normal/Abnormal for all ages).
- **Pediatric Assessment Triangle (PAT)**.
- **Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS)**.
- **Stroke Scales** (CPSS, LAPSS, RACE).
- **Trauma Scores** (Revised Trauma Score, MGAP).
- **Pain Assessment Scales** (Numeric, Wong-Baker FACES).

---

### **4. Drug Calculators**
- **IV Drip Rate Calculator** (e.g., mcg/kg/min for dopamine).
- **Pediatric Dose Calculator** (weight-based).
- **Burn Fluid Resuscitation (Parkland Formula)**.
- **APGAR Score Calculator**.
- **GCS Calculator**.
- **Anion Gap Calculator** (for metabolic acidosis).

---

### **5. Procedures & Skills Guides**
- **Step-by-Step Visual/Text Guides**:
  - Intubation (ETT, King LT, i-gel)
  - IV/IO Access
  - Needle Decompression
  - Chest Tube Monitoring (if applicable)
  - 12-Lead ECG Placement
  - Suturing/Wound Care (for critical care paramedics)
  - CPR (High-Performance, Pit Crew)
  - Defibrillation (Manual/AED)
  - Obstetrical Delivery

---

### **6. ECG & Cardiology Reference**
- **Rhythm Strip Library** (Normal/Abnormal):
  - Sinus Brady/Tachy
  - A-Fib/Flutter
  - V-Tach/V-Fib
  - Heart Blocks (1st, 2nd, 3rd)
  - PEA/Asystole
- **12-Lead ECG Interpretation**:
  - STEMI Mimics
  - Bundle Branch Blocks
  - Brugada Syndrome
  - Hyperkalemia Changes
- **Treatment Algorithms** (e.g., AHA ACLS).

---

### **7. Anatomy & Physiology**
- **Body Systems Overview** (Cardio, Neuro, Respiratory, etc.).
- **Medical Terminology** (Prefixes/Suffixes/Roots).
- **Common Abbreviations** (e.g., CP, SOB, AMS).
- **Pathophysiology of Common Diseases** (e.g., ACS, COPD, DKA).

---

### **8. Pediatric Specifics**
- **Broselow Tape Equivalent** (Weight/Length-based dosing).
- **Pediatric Vital Signs by Age**.
- **Common Pediatric Emergencies** (e.g., croup, RSV, SIDS).
- **Pediatric Resuscitation (PALS)**.

---

### **9. Trauma Guide**
- **MARCH Algorithm** (Massive hemorrhage, Airway, etc.).
- **Rapid Trauma Assessment**.
- **Burn Classification** (Rule of 9’s).
- **C-Spine Clearance Criteria** (NEXUS, Canadian C-Spine).
- **Tourniquet Application**.

---

### **10. OB/GYN Guide**
- **APGAR Scoring**.
- **Stages of Labor**.
- **Pre-Eclampsia/Eclampsia Management**.
- **Neonatal Resuscitation**.

---

### **11. Documentation & Legal**
- **SOAP Note Templates**.
- **PCR (Patient Care Report) Best Practices**.
- **Legal Considerations** (Implied Consent, DNR, HIPAA).

---

### **12. Equipment Guides**
- **Ventilator Settings** (if applicable).
- **CPAP/BiPAP Setup**.
- **Glucometer Use**.
- **IO Drill Usage**.

---

### **13. Study & Exam Prep**
- **NREMT Practice Questions** (EMT/AEMT/Paramedic).
- **Case Studies** (Real-world scenarios).
- **Flashcards** (Drugs, Terms, Protocols).
- **Skill Sheets** (NREMT Psychomotor Exam).

---

### **14. Customization & Offline Access**
- **Save Favorite Protocols**.
- **Add Local Protocols** (Customizable by agency).
- **Offline Mode** (Critical for rural areas with no signal).

---

### **15. Additional Features**
- **Search Function** (Quick access in emergencies).
- **Audio Playback** (For hands-free use).
- **Dark Mode** (For night operations).
- **Multilingual Support** (Spanish, etc.).

---

### **Example Use Case:**
A paramedic arrives at a scene with a patient in respiratory distress. They:
1. Open the app → **Respiratory Emergencies** section.
2. Assess using **OPQRST** tool.
3. Check **drug guide** for contraindications before giving albuterol.
4. Use **12-Lead ECG** reference to rule out cardiac cause.
5. Document findings via **SOAP note template**.
