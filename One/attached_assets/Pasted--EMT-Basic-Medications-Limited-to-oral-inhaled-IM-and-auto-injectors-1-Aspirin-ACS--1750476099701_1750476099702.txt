🚑 EMT (Basic) Medications
(Limited to oral, inhaled, IM, and auto-injectors)

1. Aspirin (ACS)
Dose: 324 mg PO (chewed).

Contraindications: Allergy, active GI bleed.

2. Oxygen
Dose: 2–15 L/min (NC/NRB).

Contraindications: COPD with CO2 retention (use titrated O2).

3. Epinephrine 1:1,000 (Anaphylaxis)
Dose: 0.3 mg IM (auto-injector or vial).

Contraindications: None in anaphylaxis.

4. Naloxone (Opioid OD)
Dose: 4 mg IN or 0.4 mg IM/IV (if allowed).

Contraindications: None in overdose.

5. Albuterol (Asthma/COPD)
Dose: 2.5 mg nebulized.

Contraindications: Tachycardia (use with caution).

6. Oral Glucose (Hypoglycemia)
Dose: 15–25 g PO (paste/gel).

Contraindications: Unconsciousness (use glucagon).

7. Activated Charcoal (Poisoning) (Some states)
Dose: 25–50 g PO.

Contraindications: Altered LOC, caustic ingestion.

⚡ AEMT (Advanced) Medications
(Adds IV therapy, additional IM/IN drugs)

8. Nitroglycerin (ACS/CHF)
Dose: 0.4 mg SL/spray q5min (max 3 doses).

Contraindications: SBP <90, ED medications (e.g., Viagra).

9. Glucagon (Hypoglycemia)
Dose: 1 mg IM.

Contraindications: None (if no IV access).

10. Diphenhydramine (Allergic Reactions)
Dose: 25–50 mg IV/IM.

Contraindications: Acute asthma (can thicken secretions).

11. Dextrose 50% (D50) (Hypoglycemia)
Dose: 25 g IV (pediatrics: D25 2–4 mL/kg).

Contraindications: No IV access (use glucagon).

12. Ipratropium (COPD)
Dose: 0.5 mg nebulized (with albuterol).

Contraindications: Soy/peanut allergy (rare).

💊 PARAMEDIC Medications
(Full ACLS, IV/IO, cardiac, and advanced pain control)

13. Fentanyl (Pain)
Dose: 1–2 mcg/kg IV/IN (max 100 mcg/dose).

Contraindications: Severe respiratory depression.

14. Morphine (Pain/ACS)
Dose: 2–5 mg IV q5–10min (max 10 mg).

Contraindications: Hypotension, head injury.

15. Ketamine (Pain/Sedation)
Dose:

Pain: 0.1–0.3 mg/kg IV.

Sedation: 1–2 mg/kg IV (RSI).

Contraindications: Schizophrenia, uncontrolled HTN.

16. Amiodarone (VF/VT)
Dose: 300 mg IV (repeat 150 mg if refractory).

Contraindications: Bradycardia, Torsades.

17. Dopamine (Shock)
Dose: 5–20 mcg/kg/min IV drip.

Contraindications: Tachyarrhythmias.

18. RSI Medications (e.g., Succinylcholine, Etomidate)
Dose: Protocol-specific (e.g., succinylcholine 1.5 mg/kg IV).

Contraindications: Hyperkalemia, MH risk (succinylcholine).
